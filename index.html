About Us

Kwality industrial Tools & Hardwares is a leading venture came into existence in the year 1990 engaged in the business of hardware and tools for different industrial and commercial sectors. With our business offices based at Chennai, Tamil Nadu (India)

our company is engaged in executing its entire business activities as a Sole Proprietorship based business. We are engaged in the trading, dealing and supplying of an extensive range of products such as Gas Welding Rod, Plain Spring Washer, Drill Bit, Drilling Machine and many more. Manufactured by our vendors keeping the quality standards as a major focus, our range of products is in extensive demand amongst our valued customers.
